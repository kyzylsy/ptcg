#### Release Info
The Vending Machine (Japanese: 拡張シート Expansion Sheet) cards are a series of special collections for the Pokémon Trading Card Game released in Japan. 
The Expansion Sheet 3 was released on November 24th, 1998. It features eighteen sheets with four cards per-sheet along with cardboard damage counters and coins and the main color of this series is green.
