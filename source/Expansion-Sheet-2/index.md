#### Release Info
The Vending Machine (Japanese: 拡張シート Expansion Sheet) cards are a series of special collections for the Pokémon Trading Card Game released in Japan. 
The Expansion Sheet 2 was released on June 17th, 1998. It features eighteen sheets with three cards per-sheet along with cardboard damage counters and coins and the main color of this series is red.
